Battle Records

Welcome Pokémon Trainers! This application will be a battle simulator where you may choose 1 of 6 pokemon. In this application your attacks are randomized in that you can either miss, get a super effective attack, not very effective attack and a normal attack. To finish the battle, either you or the opponent must have their HP reduced from 100 to 0. Now you should be ready to begin your first battle. Click the button at the top to begin and if needed, feel free to look at our github profiles at the bottom of the page. Thank you and battle on!
